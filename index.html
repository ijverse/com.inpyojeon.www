about

Inpyo Jeon is a Korean translator and comic book writer. He has translated 30+ books including 20+ comic books before writing his first graphic novel, Batman: Saba for DC Comics in 2020.

In 2021, Inpyo Jeon wrote and illustrated Heavy Rain, a post-apocalyptic fiction published by Sigongsa.

Jeon is better known as an one-sixth scale action figure sculptor and painter(IG: @ij.workshop). His workshop is located at Seongnam, Korea.
